Frequently Asked Questions

How does Senti.co calculate sentiment scores?

We teach the computer to understand human emotion. Senti.co uses a carefully developed and highly secure system that processes text to accurately categorize the core feeling (Positive, Negative, or Neutral). It's far more accurate than just searching for simple keywords.

What languages does Senti.co support?

Currently, our primary focus is on English, where our accuracy is highest. We are actively testing and expanding our support to include other major languages, such as Spanish, German, and French.

Is my data secure and private?

Your security and privacy are our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption for all data transfer. Furthermore, user account details and passwords are securely hashed using a robust backend (Flask and MySQL) before they are stored.
